## About Me ✨

I am **Zhongyao Tuo**, a Member of the Communist Party of China and an undergraduate student majoring in Intelligent Science and Technology at the College of Automation & College of Artificial Intelligence, Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications (NJUPT), with a **ranking of 1/62**. I have served as Academic Monitor, Deputy League Branch Secretary, Student Advisor, Student Assistant in the University Office Secretariat, and Teaching Information Officer in the Academic Affairs Office. I graduated as an Outstanding Graduate and was recommended for postgraduate studies at the School of Information Science and Technology, **ShanghaiTech University**, pursuing a Master's degree in Information and Communication Engineering.

During my undergraduate studies, I focused on interdisciplinary research in **Embodied AI** and **Embedded Systems**. As a core member, I participated in the development of the **AI+Quantum Teaching Machine** (the world's first, reported by Guangming Daily and Xinhua Daily), and led the project **"Electromagnetic Fire Extinguishing Vehicle Based on Stereo Thermal Imaging."** I have published a total of **8 academic papers** (2 as first author, 3 as second author, 3 as third author), with 2 additional papers under review, and participated in **1 patent application**. I won the **National Gold Award** in the 9th China "Internet+" College Students Innovation and Entrepreneurship Competition, the Second Prize in the Regional Competition of the 19th National College Student Intelligent Car Competition, an Honorable Mention in the 2024 American Mathematical Modeling Competition, and a total of **3 national-level awards**, **3 provincial-level awards**, and **17 university-level honors** including the Huawei Intelligent Base "Future Star," President's Commendation, First-Class Scholarship, Outstanding Student Leader, and Outstanding Young Volunteer.

Beyond research, I actively participate in social practice, including the 10th National Memorial Ceremony for Nanjing Massacre Victims, the 2nd Xianlin University Town Art Festival, and the 27th "Tianyi" Culture and Art Festival. I have led multiple "three visits to the countryside" teams to Hubei and Jiangsu provinces, delivering science popularization lectures on over ten occasions.

> **"Aim high, stand firm, climb step by step, flourish and thrive."** — This is my guiding principle, both for my academic journey and my life ahead.

## Research Interests 🔬

I am currently focused on the cutting-edge interdisciplinary field of **Embodied AI**, with specific research interests including:

- **Robot Autonomous Navigation and Decision-Making**: Perception, mapping, localization, and path planning in complex unknown environments
- **Embedded System Design and Optimization**: Hardware-software co-design for robotic platforms based on FPGA, ARM, and other hardware platforms
- **Multi-Sensor Information Fusion**: Fused perception and state estimation from vision, ultrasonic, inertial, and other multi-source data
- **Hardware-Software Co-Designed Embodied Large Models**: Exploring efficient integration of embodied large model algorithms with embedded systems
