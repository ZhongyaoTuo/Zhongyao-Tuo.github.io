## Featured Projects

### AI+Quantum Teaching Machine (World's First)

**Duration**: Jun 2023 – Jun 2025  
**Role**: Core Contributor (Deep Learning Algorithm Development & Imaging Optimization)

Conducted predictive modeling and imaging of static magnetic fields and microwave magnetic fields on chip surfaces based on deep learning and diamond NV color center quantum systems. Responsible for deep learning algorithm development and imaging program optimization; assisted in the development of the teaching machine's automation and circuit systems. Successfully achieved breakthroughs in magnetic measurement technology through deep learning, leading to the world's first AI+Quantum teaching machine, widely reported by **Xinhua Daily** and **Guangming Daily**.

**Achievements**: Published 4 papers as first author or student-first author, filed 1 patent, won over thirty awards including the Second Prize in the Embedded Competition (East China Division).

### Electromagnetic Fire Extinguishing Vehicle Based on Stereo Thermal Imaging

**Duration**: Sep 2022 – Jun 2024  
**Role**: Lead

Developed a maze-algorithm-based intelligent vehicle to traverse target areas, equipped with an electromagnetic cannon to extinguish fires when abnormal heat sources are detected. Responsible for embedded algorithm development and electromagnetic cannon module design; coordinated technology transfer. The project won over thirty awards including the Second Prize in the Embedded Competition (East China Division).

**Related Publication**:
- Z. Tuo, S. Jiang, X. Huang, et al. Design of Electromagnetically Driven Fire Extinguishing Bomb Based on Stereo Thermal Imaging. *College Physics Experiment*, 2024, 37(1)

### Micromouse Design Based on Real Maze Terrain

**Duration**: 2023  
**Role**: Core Member

Designed a micromouse capable of autonomously navigating real maze environments using ultrasonic distance sensing and traditional maze-solving algorithms, suitable for complex terrains with variable path widths and multiple dead ends.

**Related Publication**:
- R. Wu, Z. Tuo, W. Liu, et al. Design of a Micromouse Based on Real Maze Terrain. *College Physics Experiment*, 2023, 36(3)

### Portable PWM Square Wave Signal Generator Based on FPGA

**Duration**: 2023 – 2024  
**Role**: First Author

Designed a portable PWM square wave signal generator based on FPGA, with duty cycle adjustable in 1% steps and frequency controlled via push buttons achieving 100 Hz, 1 kHz, and 10 μs minimum resolution.

**Related Publication**:
- Z. Tuo, X. Huang, Z. Li, et al. Design of a Portable PWM Square Wave Signal Generator Based on FPGA. *College Physics Experiment*, 2024, 37(4)

### Anti-Strong-Interference Data Transmission Based on Improved Interleaving Algorithm

**Duration**: 2024  
**Role**: First Author

Proposed an improved interleaving algorithm for reliable data transmission under strong electromagnetic interference, enhancing the anti-interference capability of UART communication in harsh electromagnetic environments.

**Related Publication**:
- Z. Tuo, N. Hu, X. Huang, et al. Anti-Strong-Interference Data Transmission Based on Improved Interleaving Algorithm. *College Physics Experiment*, 2024, 37(3)
